# TRINITY REQUIRED PIECES

To classify a repository or patch as **Complete up to Consumer Declaration**, the following evidence must be present:

1. **Trinity Core reference** is present (package/module/import path).
2. **Registry/manifest** exists and is referenced by the Trinity mount.
3. **EnvironmentRenderer contract** exists or a documented mount call is present.
4. **Akira Codex gating reference** exists for timeline access.
5. **Timeline doors** (NETERU / SIGNAL ERA / RVP) are referenced or explicitly marked as gated/locked.
6. **Read-only (consumer-only) declaration** is present.
